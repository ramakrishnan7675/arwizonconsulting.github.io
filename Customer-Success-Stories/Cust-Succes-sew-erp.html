Sales Cloud and Integration


About The Project :


This implementation was for a company providing a wide range of industrial sewing machines in the Utah region. The customer was using QuickBooks as their single platform to maintain customer data and order data


Scope Of The Project :


  • Auto Sync QuickBooks to salesforce platform
  • Create a web-to-lead form, attach to a lead queue, and enable lead assignment
  • Notify the sales representative
  • Reports to show new orders & products not sold for the given period
  • Online Payments
  • Location Rental, Location assignment to Vendors

Challenges :


  • Consulting for business process change
  • Integration of Salesforce to QB

    • Solution Implemented :


  • The customer and order data from QuickBooks are retrieved into a csv file and then automatically synchronized into salesforce platform
  • In the same way the Customer and orders created in salesforce are synchronized into QuickBooks account
  • When new orders get imported weekly from QuickBooks, get active customers and set a follow up task for customers that haven’t recently ordered for a certain period and assign it to respective sales representative
  • Create reports in salesforce to analyze new orders
  • Syncing of QB data with Salesforce application
  • Notify Sales representatives about customers who haven’t placed orders over a specific period

    Sales Force
