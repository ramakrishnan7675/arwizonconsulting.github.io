Product Costing Tool


About The Project :


The customer is a leading manufacturer of ride control equipment and has his presence in the continents of America and Asia. The company had a high cycle of quote generation and was unable to keep track of costing at various stages of NPD.


Scope Of The Project :


  • Reduce cycle time for quote generation
  • Keep track of various costs added during NPD that happen due to design changes
  • Get necessary inputs from various stake holders using workflows
  • Dashboard to track the quote status

Challenges :


  • Image Processing
  • Capturing Product – Generating Bill of Material related to the product using Einstein Prediction
  • Approval Process
  • Quote Generation – OOB utilized

    • Solution Implemented :


  • Capability to collect the required product related data from different platforms
  • Sales team will post the enquiry details along with product category information in the PLC tool
  • PLC tool will present the list of nearest matched products against each enquiry / product keyword
  • Research and Development (R & D) team will pick the most suitable matched product and customize it further PLC tool will automatically map the respective workflow (template) to the product selected based on product category and manufacturing location / business unit / plants
  • Based on the workflow, product will pass through different stages for approval


    • Technology Stack :


    Einstein Vision (Image Classification, Object Detection, Optical Character Recognition)
    Lightning App
